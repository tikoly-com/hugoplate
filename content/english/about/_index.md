**tikolyDAO** is the DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization) dedicated to innovate around sustainable tech, including decentralized data networks, renewable energy, and next gen energy+digital infrastructure such as [DuoNB](https://duonb.org).

_tikoly_ has been a member project of [Breakpoints.live / bpDAO](https://breakpoints.live/join) and [_DuoNB Foundation_](https://duonb.org).

> tikolyDAO was inspired and co-founded with [DuoNB Foundation](https://duonb.org), the tech alliance dedicated to DuoNB, [the micro-framework that converges renewable energy, optical networking and content for the new sustainable urbanization.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XpTVB7v1NBI&t=190s)
> 
> tikolyDAO HAVE NOT minted NOR will issue any equity (or tokens) by tikolyDAO Charter. We enable partners or clients to do so, instead.
