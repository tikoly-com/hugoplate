**tikolyDAO** is the DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization) dedicated to innovate around sustainable tech,AI and Web3. tikolyDAO's equity is governed by specific projects' own DAOs under the umbrella _tikolyDAO_, governed by leading professionals in the fields around the world, include crypto and web3 investors.

> _tikoly_ is inspired and co-founded with [DuoNB Foundation](https://duonb.org), the tech alliance dedicated to DuoNB, [the micro-framework that converges renewable energy, optical networking and content for the new sustainable urbanization.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XpTVB7v1NBI&t=190s)
