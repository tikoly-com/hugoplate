Tikoly is a DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization) dedicated to innovation around sustainable tech and Web3. Tikoly equity is governed by _TikolyDAO_, owned and governed by a core team, and a global network of participating partners.

> _tikoly_ is inspired and co-founded with [DuoNB Foundation](https://duonb.org), the tech alliance dedicated to DuoNB, [the micro-framework that converges renewable energy, optical networking and content for sustainable homes](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XpTVB7v1NBI&t=190s).
