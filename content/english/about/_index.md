**tikolyDAO** is a DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization) dedicated to innovation around sustainable tech,AI and Web3. tikolyDAO's equity is governed by specific project under the umbrella _tikolyDAO_, governed by a core team, and some are supported by invited investors.

> _tikoly_ is inspired and co-founded with [DuoNB Foundation](https://duonb.org), the tech alliance dedicated to DuoNB, [the micro-framework that converges renewable energy, optical networking and content for sustainable homes](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XpTVB7v1NBI&t=190s).
