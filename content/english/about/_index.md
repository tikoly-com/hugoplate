**tikolyDAO** is the DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization) dedicated to innovate around sustainable tech, renewable energy, and next gen energy+digital infrastructure, with global partners such as [DuoNB](https://duonb.org).

tikolyDAO has NOT yet minted or issued any equity (or tokens) , and it's been a member project of [Breakpoints.live / bpDAO](https://breakpoints.live/join) and [_DuoNB Foundation_](https://duonb.org).

> tikolyDAO was inspired and co-founded with [DuoNB Foundation](https://duonb.org), the tech alliance dedicated to DuoNB, [the micro-framework that converges renewable energy, optical networking and content for the new sustainable urbanization.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XpTVB7v1NBI&t=190s)
