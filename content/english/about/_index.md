**tikolyDAO** is the DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization) dedicated to innovate around sustainable tech, renewable energy, and next gen energy+digital infrastructure, such as [DuoNB](https://duonb.org).

tikolyDAO has NOT yet minted or issued any equity (or tokens) , and its member projects are governed by their respective DAOs under the umbrella _tikolyDAO_.

> _tikoly_ is inspired and co-founded with [DuoNB Foundation](https://duonb.org), the tech alliance dedicated to DuoNB, [the micro-framework that converges renewable energy, optical networking and content for the new sustainable urbanization.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XpTVB7v1NBI&t=190s)
